Our Faq

At Debuggers, we understand that navigating software development can raise many questions.
Whether you're seeking technical support or insights into our services, we’re here to help you with clear answers and expert guidance!

1. What types of web development services do you offer?

+

We provide a wide range of web development services including custom websites, responsive web design, single-page applications (SPA), content management systems (CMS) like WordPress, and enterprise web applications.

2. Can you develop a custom e-commerce store tailored to my business needs?

+

Yes, we specialize in building custom e-commerce stores with features like product management, payment gateway integration, shopping carts, and advanced analytics tailored to your specific business requirements.

3. What technologies do you use for web development?

+

Our team works with modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue.js), PHP, Laravel, Node.js, Python, and more to create robust and scalable web applications.

4. Do you provide ongoing support after the project is completed?

+

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your website or e-commerce store runs smoothly, including security updates, performance optimization, and troubleshooting.

5. How much time does it take to develop an e-commerce store?

+

The timeline for developing an e-commerce store depends on the complexity of the project. A basic store may take 4–6 weeks, while more advanced custom stores with multiple features could take 8–12 weeks or longer.

6. What is the process of working with your graphics design team?

+

Our graphics design process begins with understanding your brand and project goals. We then create initial design concepts and refine them based on your feedback. Once finalized, we deliver the designs in the required formats for use in web, print, or digital media.

7. Can you redesign an existing website or e-commerce store?

+

Yes, we offer website and e-commerce store redesign services to improve user experience (UX), enhance functionality, and ensure the site is optimized for mobile and search engines.

8. What kind of graphics design services do you provide?

+

Our graphics design services include logo design, branding, UI/UX design, marketing materials (brochures, flyers), social media graphics, and website visuals. We ensure your visuals reflect your brand identity and engage your target audience.

9. How do you ensure my website or e-commerce store is secure?

+

We implement best security practices including SSL encryption, secure coding techniques, regular updates, and strong authentication mechanisms to safeguard your website or e-commerce store from vulnerabilities.

10. Can you integrate third-party tools and services into my website or e-commerce store?

+

Absolutely! We can integrate a variety of third-party services such as payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), shipping providers, CRMs, marketing tools, and more to enhance the functionality of your website or e-commerce store.

11. Do you offer branding and logo design services?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive branding services, including logo design, color schemes, typography, and overall brand identity development. We work closely with you to create a cohesive and memorable brand that resonates with your target audience.

12. Can you create custom illustrations and icons for my website or marketing materials?

+

Absolutely! Our team can design custom illustrations, icons, and other visual elements tailored to your brand. These assets can be used across websites, mobile apps, marketing materials, and social media to enhance the visual appeal and user experience.
